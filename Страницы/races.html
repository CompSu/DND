Playable Races

Human

In most worlds, humans are the youngest of the common races. They came late to the world stage and live much shorter lives than dwarves, elves, and dragons. Perhaps it is the shortness of their lives that drives them to achieve as much as they can in the time they have. Or perhaps they have something to prove to the elder races, building mighty empires based on conquest and trade.

Elf

With their unearthly grace and delicate features, elves appear almost too beautiful to humans and other races. They are slightly shorter than humans, ranging from 5 to 6 feet (150 to 185 centimeters), and weigh between 100 and 145 pounds (45 to 65 kilograms).

Dwarf

Kingdoms full of ancient grandeur, palaces carved into mountains, and loyalty to clan unite all dwarves. They are known for their traditions and a burning hatred of goblins and orcs.

Halfling

The goal of most halflings is a home—a place to settle down in peace and quiet. While some live in remote farming communities, others are wanderers drawn by the open road and the wonders of new places.

Dragonborn

Born of dragons, dragonborn walk proudly through a world that greets them with fear and misunderstanding. They were originally hatched from dragon eggs, combining the best qualities of dragons and humanoids.

Tiefling

To be a tiefling is to be constantly stared at and whispered about, suffering from the effects of a pact made generations ago that allows Asmodeus to contribute to their bloodline.
