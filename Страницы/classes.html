Playable Classes

Fighter

Members of perhaps the most diverse class in the D&D world. Knights errant, conquering warlords, royal champions, elite infantry, armored mercenaries, and bandit kings - as warriors, they are all masters of weapons, armor, and combat. They are also no strangers to death - they bear it themselves, and often look into its cold eyes.

Wizard

Wizards are adepts of the higher mage, grouped together by the type of spell they cast. Drawing on the subtle weaves of magic that permeate the universe, wizards are capable of casting spells of explosive fire, sparking lightning, subtle deception, and crude mind control. Their magic summons monsters from other planes of existence, predicts the future, and turns defeated enemies into zombies. Their most powerful spells can transmute one substance into another, call down meteorites from the sky, and open portals to other worlds.

Cleric

Clerics are intermediaries between the mortal world and the distant realms of the gods. As diverse as the gods they serve, priests embody the work of their deities. Unlike the average preacher, a priest is imbued with divine magic.

Rogue

Rogues rely on skill, stealth, and enemy vulnerabilities to gain the upper hand in any situation. They have the dexterity to find a solution to any situation, demonstrating the resourcefulness and flexibility that are the cornerstones of any successful adventuring party.

Barbarian

Despite their diversity, all barbarians have one thing in common: their rage. Unbridled, unquenchable, and mindless anger. More than just an emotion, their rage is like the ferocity of a cornered predator, like the merciless blow of a hurricane, like the storm surge of the ocean. Some of them rage comes from communing with ferocious animal spirits. Others draw it from anger at a reality full of pain and suffering. But for every barbarian, rage is the source not only of battle madness, but also of incredible reflexes, resilience, and unrivaled strength.

Bard

Whether a scholar, a skald, or a rogue, a bard weaves magic from words and music to inspire allies, demoralize foes, manipulate minds, create illusions, and even heal wounds.
