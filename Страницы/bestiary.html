Avatar of Death

A spectral humanoid skeleton dressed in tattered black robes and wielding a spectral scythe. It appears within 10 feet of you (in a location of the DM's choosing) and attacks you, warning everyone that you must win this battle alone. The death avatar fights until you are killed or it drops to 0 hit points, at which point it disappears. If anyone tries to help you, they will summon their own death avatar. A creature killed by the death avatar cannot be brought back to life.
